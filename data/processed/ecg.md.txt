Effective Communication Guidelines (ECG)Communicate your assumptions - verbally or in writing.Use direct communication and involve everyone concerned.When in doubt, make and communicate recommendations and decisions. (Don't wait for someone else to)Processes are there for a reason: If something is not working as per the agreed to process, ASAP communication is a mustCommunication, in almost all cases, needs to come before a "solution".Understand the perspective of the end-user: If in doubt or unsure, communicate and ask questions.
